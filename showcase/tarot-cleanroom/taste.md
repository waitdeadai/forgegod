# Tarot Showcase Taste Contract

This file is the visual gate for the definitive ForgeGod tarot showcase.

Non-negotiables:

- Read as a premium ForgeGod artifact, not a generic mystical tarot site.
- Primary look: dark graphite surfaces, electric cyan structure, disciplined white type.
- Allowed symbolic language: pyramid, wireframe triangle, halo ring, central `1`, ritual countdowns, geometric card backs.
- Forbidden look: stock occult motifs, purple-on-black mysticism, cluttered gradients, generic neon cyberpunk, childish UI, glossy app-store fluff.
- Mobile quality is part of taste. If it breaks at `320px`, it fails taste.

Brand direction:

- Core palette:
  - background `#0c0c0c`
  - surface `#141414`
  - surface-alt `#1b1b1f`
  - cyan `#44d9f3`
  - white `#f5f7fb`
  - muted `#8c95a1`
- Halo accent is allowed only in restrained ceremonial moments, never as broad decorative gold wash.
- Typography should feel editorial and deliberate, not mystical cosplay.

Tarot-specific guidance:

- Card backs should use ForgeGod symbolism, not rider-waite pastiche.
- The ritual gate should feel precise, clockwork, and engineered.
- Motion should feel intentional and sacred, not noisy.
- Reading steps should feel like a guided instrument panel with atmosphere.

Reject if:

- the result could be mistaken for a random tarot template
- the color system drifts away from ForgeGod brand
- there is horizontal overflow, cramped touch targets, or weak hierarchy
- the reveal flow lacks ceremony or the landing lacks product confidence
