Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Smoothrevenue different from hiring an in-house AI team?

In-house AI teams require 6-12 months to hire, cost $800K-2M annually in salaries, and need time to understand your domain. We bring pre-built enterprise playbooks, validated across Fortune 500 deployments, that can be operational within weeks. You're not paying us to learn— you're paying us because we've already learned at scale.

How do you handle data security and compliance?

Security isn't an afterthought— it's architectural. We implement enterprise-grade isolation, SOC 2 compliant workflows, and domain-specific compliance frameworks for financial services, healthcare, and regulated industries. Every deployment includes audit logging, access controls, and data residency options. We provide documentation for your legal and compliance teams.

What's your pricing model and ROI expectations?

We structure engagements around outcome milestones, not time-and-materials. Typical projects range from $150K-500K for MVP through production. ROI targets are built into our contracts— we don't succeed unless you do. Most clients see positive ROI within 6-9 months through automation gains, error reduction, and accelerated decision-making.

Do you work with our existing Claude accounts and vendors?

Yes. We integrate with existing Anthropic accounts and help optimize token usage and costs. Our consultants are vendor-agnostic— we recommend the right tools for your specific needs, whether that's Claude, other LLMs, or hybrid approaches. We don't lock you into proprietary frameworks.

What does the typical engagement timeline look like?

Discovery takes 2 weeks. MVP delivery in 6-8 weeks. Full deployment in 12-16 weeks. We front-load validation— if an approach won't work, we identify it in the first month, not the third. For urgent needs, we have accelerated programs that can deliver working prototypes in 4 weeks.

How do you measure success and report progress?

Every engagement has defined KPIs tied to business outcomes, not vanity metrics. We provide weekly progress reports with technical dashboards, monthly business impact reviews, and quarterly executive summaries. Our success criteria are transparent and measurable— you see exactly what we see.

What industries do you specialize in?

Financial services, healthcare, logistics, and enterprise SaaS with complex compliance requirements. We focus on industries where AI failures have real consequences— where accuracy, auditability, and reliability aren't optional. These are exactly the industries where enterprise-grade implementation matters most.

What happens after deployment— do you provide ongoing support?

Yes. We don't disappear after go-live. All engagements include 90 days of hypercare support, documentation for your internal teams, and knowledge transfer sessions. Most clients stay with us because the value compounds over time.
