Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Smoothrevenue different from hiring an in-house AI team?

In-house AI teams require 6-12 months to hire, cost $800K-2M annually in salaries (based on 4-8 ML engineers at $150-250K each plus recruiting overhead), and need 3-6 months to understand your domain. We deploy in 8-12 weeks because we bring pre-built integration playbooks validated across 40+ Fortune 500 deployments, production-tested prompt libraries for your industry, and infrastructure templates that eliminate 60% of boilerplate work. You're not paying us to learn— you're paying us because we've already solved the same problems at 5-10 companies in your sector.

How do you handle data security and compliance?

Security isn't an afterthought— it's architectural. We deploy tenant-isolated VPCs with SOC 2 Type II controls, encrypt data at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3), and map compliance frameworks per industry (HIPAA for healthcare, PCI-DSS for payments, SOX for financial reporting). Every deployment includes immutable audit logs, RBAC with SSO integration, and EU/US data residency options. We deliver a security documentation package for your legal and compliance teams within the first sprint.

What's your pricing model and ROI expectations?

We price by outcome milestones, not hourly rates. A typical engagement runs $150K-500K from MVP through production. Example: a $280K compliance automation project delivered $890K in annual savings (3.2x ROI in year one). We define 3-4 payment milestones tied to measurable outcomes (e.g., "90% accuracy on test set," "<5s p95 latency in production"). If we don't hit the milestone, you don't pay that tranche.

Do you work with our existing Claude accounts and vendors?

Yes. We integrate with your existing Anthropic account via your own API keys. A recent client reduced Claude API costs by 38% ($14K/month savings) by implementing prompt caching and batched inference— changes we identified in the first architecture review. We also connect to your existing data stack (Snowflake, Databricks, AWS S3) without requiring new vendors.

What does the typical engagement timeline look like?

Discovery takes 2 weeks. MVP delivery in 6-8 weeks. Full deployment in 12-16 weeks. We front-load validation— if an approach won't work, we identify it in the first month, not the third. For urgent needs, we have accelerated programs that can deliver working prototypes in 4 weeks.

How do you measure success and report progress?

Every engagement tracks 3-5 KPIs agreed on before work starts. Examples from recent projects: time-to-first-response (target: <15 min), claims processing cost (target: <$10/claim), document retrieval accuracy (target: >92%). We deliver weekly Slack/email updates with dashboard screenshots showing actual vs. target metrics. If we miss a sprint target, you know by Friday— not the next quarterly review.

What industries do you specialize in?

Financial services (banking, insurance, capital markets), healthcare (payers, providers, pharma), logistics (freight, last-mile, cold chain), and SaaS companies with >$50M ARR and complex compliance requirements. We focus on industries where AI failures have real consequences— where a wrong prediction means regulatory fines, patient safety risks, or multi-million-dollar supply chain disruptions. That specificity is why our implementations achieve 94% first-pass accuracy vs. the industry average of 72%.

What happens after deployment— do you provide ongoing support?

All engagements include 90 days of hypercare: a named engineer on your Slack channel, 4-hour response SLA for production issues, and weekly 30-min optimization reviews. We hand off runbooks, architecture decision records, and a recorded 4-session training program for your internal team. 87% of clients extend to a retainer because the model accuracy improves 8-15% in the first 6 months post-launch through ongoing prompt tuning.
